Offline

Maps and Navigation

Create Maps and have them ready always, even without Internet connection.

Description

Maps

Offline allows you to create maps that will work on their own, no connection needed.

Ideal for remote places, exploring nature, or traveling overseas.

 

GPS

Unleash the power of the GPS already on your device.

It is completely independent from your Internet connection and works at any place in the World.

 

Directions

Highlight the points of interest on your map to mark the start and end of a route to get directions.

Once created, directions work completely offline.

 

Navigation

Get navigation for walking and driving.

 

Share

Share your maps and directions with others, simple, fast.

 

Offload

Save disk space in your device and store your maps on the cloud, ready for download when you need them again.
