Unpromoted

Filter your job search on LinkedIn

Description

Unpromoted is an app and a Safari extension for macOS.

With Unpromoted you can make your job search on LinkedIn significantly more efficient, faster, and arguable easier.

This is achieved by adding filters to remove Promoted job ads from your search on LinkedIn, as most of the time these ads are totally unrelated to what you are looking for.

It also allows you to filter-out specific companies that you do not wish to see anymore, as some job ads just keep popping-up, and there is no real way to hide them.

We believe these features should be built-in on LinkedIn itself, and experience has taught us Promoted job ads are just a hindrance while searching for a job; something we could honestly do without when job hunting.

...but in the meantime, you can have those features with Unpromoted.

 

Features

  • Filter promoted jobs
  • Filter jobs by company

 

Pricing

Unpromoted is maintained by voluntary contributions. If you wish to do so you can sponsor it, directly in the app, with In-App Purchases. Sponsoring is a 1 time only purchase.

Other than that, Unpromoted is completely free to use fully featured.
