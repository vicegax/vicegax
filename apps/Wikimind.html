Wikimind

Wikipedia mind map

Description

Learn something new every day.

Wikimind is an visualisation tool for Wikipedia.

You can generate knowledge mind maps with articles and concepts that are of your interest.

Easily jump from reading a subject to the next one, and all others related to it; follow your own Rabbit Hole and backtrack your trail to keep exploring different paths. Just like a neural network of information.

Some articles contain points of interest and locations, and you can easily visualise them on a map, all this without leaving the app.

Learn something new every day and make Wikimind your favourite educational app.
